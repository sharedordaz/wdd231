Study Circle Website Plan

Site Name

Study Circle

This name represents a recurring, inclusive study group that meets weekly for discussions, learning, and sharing educational content online through their YouTube channel.

Optional domain availability: studycirclehub.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a central place for study group members and guests to view weekly meeting topics, access links to the latest YouTube videos, and join future discussions. It also offers resources like recommended readings, a contact form, and group values.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the latest video from this week's study session?
  • How can I join next Wednesday’s group discussion?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Blue — Used for headings, buttons, and links.

Secondary Color: Light Gray — Used for background and section dividers.

Typography

Headings: Georgia – Provides a professional and academic feel.

Body Text: Segoe UI – Clean, easy to read on screens.
