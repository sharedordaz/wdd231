Our Values:

  • Respect all participants and their opinions.
  • Honor the beliefs of the different members of the study group.
  • Uphold reverence and dignity during our meetings.
  • Be inclusive and kind to everyone, regardless of their background or beliefs.
  • Encourage questions and honest seeking in a spirit of humility.
  • Avoid contentious discussions; seek to build unity and love.
  • Support one another in learning and new principles.
  • Respect sacred topics and handle them with care and reverence.
  • Be punctual and prepared to participate thoughtfully.
  • Respect the beliefs of other faiths and promote interfaith understanding.
